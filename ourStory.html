Our Story

Our passion for beautiful food and the way it can bring people together is what we love.

We are an independent Australian family owned business and for over 18 years we have been introducing and delivering specialty foods that customers may find hard to find at their local retailers. We want our online grocery store to be your one-stop shop for all things gorgeously gourmet. After many years in the corporate world, it was the best decision to work for ourselves instead of someone else. To create & build something that we are passionate about instead of working hard to fulfil someone else's goals is extremely satisfying.

Specialties Band Products

We proudly support Australian boutique and regional producers and are passionate about bringing you the best quality products these manufacturers have to offer. While our philosophy is to source products locally, we also include some special imported products in our range so we can offer the best from the international community. Oh, and we sneak in a few essential kitchenware items aswell.
